The Very Berry Mojito

By Yona Kimball-Smith
Prep:
10 minutes
Yield:
2 drinks
  • Shortcut: What to Buy
  • Shortcut: How to Make

Ingredients

Most enjoyable with fresh ingredients!

  • 2 tablespoons fresh raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons fresh blackberries
  • 4 lime wedges
  • 6 mint leaves
  • 4 ounces rum
  • seltzer water
  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup

Directions

How to prepare the drinks

  1. Take two short cocktail glasses and fill with equal parts blackberries, raspberries, mint leaves, and simple syrup.
  2. Squeeze 1 lime wedge into each glass and then pour in equal parts rum.
  3. Muddle the mixture together breaking up the berries and bruising the mint.
  4. Add ice and top it off with seltzer.
  5. Gently stir all ingredients to combine.

Hope you enjoy!
